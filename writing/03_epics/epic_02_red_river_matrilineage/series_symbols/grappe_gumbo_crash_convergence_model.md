# Grappe Gumbo — multi-angle “Crash” convergence model

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Develop **independent angles** now, allow them to **accelerate separately**, then **collide** in the final novel (Book 09 staging) like *Crash*: multiple storylines that were always emotionally connected become **simultaneously unavoidable**.

**Gumbo / sassafras / filé** is the **family emblem** — not a cute motif, but a **proof** that the line remembers through practice.

## Angle bank (develop independently)

| Angle | What it carries in scenes | Example echo surfaces |
|---|---|---|
| Plant / land | where leaves are gathered; seasonality; permission to pick | maps, walks, boundary knowledge |
| Women’s medicine / care | broths, recovery, feeding the sick | healing without speechifying |
| Child learning | wrong leaves, shame, pride | cohort scenes |
| Trade / exchange | what enters the pot from outside | visitors, stores, scarcity |
| Law / politics | public rules vs kitchen rules | later books; modern public life |
| Labor / scarcity | who stirs, who decides | Depression household density |
| Identity / naming | when “filé” language arrives | colonial translation pressure |
| Public persona vs private pot | who is watched | Book 08–09 |
| Archive vs pot | records lie; the pot tells | late collision |
| Survival technology | food as infrastructure | war/reconstruction |
| Grief / celebration | funeral pot vs wedding pot | careful separation from “wedding fantasy” in Book 01 |

## Collision design rules (late epic)

- Collisions must be **earned** by prior independent set-ups — no single chapter “explains gumbo.”
- The collision is not only **informational**; it must be **relational** (who is in the room, who is excluded, who feeds whom).

## Research gate

Nothing in this model authorizes **unverified** ethnographic claims on-page. The model is **staging discipline**, not a license to assert.
