# Grappe Gumbo — multi-angle “Crash” convergence model

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Develop **independent angles** now, allow them to **accelerate separately**, then **collide** in the final novel (Book 09 staging) like *Crash*: multiple storylines that were always emotionally connected become **simultaneously unavoidable**.

**Gumbo / sassafras / filé** is the **family emblem** — not a cute motif, but a **proof** that the line remembers through practice.

## Collision design rules (late epic)

- Collisions must be **earned** by prior independent set-ups — no single chapter “explains gumbo.”
- The collision is not only **informational**; it must be **relational** (who is in the room, who is excluded, who feeds whom).

## Research gate

Nothing in this model authorizes **unverified** ethnographic claims on-page. The model is **staging discipline**, not a license to assert.
