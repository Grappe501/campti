# Gumbo Three-Influences Symbol

Canon status: PROPOSED pending research

## Governing idea

**True Grappe Gumbo** becomes a metaphor for the epic itself.

It is not one origin. It is layered inheritance.

## Three secret foundations

| Influence | Ingredient / Method | Symbolic role | Era to enter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Native | sassafras / filé / healing soup knowledge | land knowledge, women’s medicine, memory | earliest |
| French | roux | colonial adaptation, technique, transformation | later |
| African | okra soup / gombo influence | survival, thickness, African presence in Louisiana foodways | later |

## Rule

Do not collapse these influences into one simplistic origin story.

Let each enter when historically and culturally appropriate.

## Epic function

Gumbo becomes:

- food
- metaphor
- family memory
- survival technology
- blended history
- a way the family carries what records forget
