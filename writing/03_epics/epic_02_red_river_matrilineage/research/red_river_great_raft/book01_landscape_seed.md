# Book 01 landscape seed — Great Raft

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

In Book 01, the Great Raft appears only as part of **life and landscape**.

Do not explain future port history.

Do not name Shreve.

Do not mention Shreveport.

Do not foreshadow Campti’s later port loss directly.

## Book 01 use

The raft / logjam world may appear as:

- backed water
- blocked channels
- tree-choked passages
- lake-like stretches
- slow water
- fish habitat
- travel obstacle
- navigation knowledge
- children learning where boats cannot go
- elders reading water movement
- trade routes shaped by obstruction
- landscape that feels ancient and normal

## Rule

Book 01 characters do **not** know the future economic consequences.

The reader should experience the Great Raft as **natural geography** and **lived reality**.

Later books may reveal that this landscape shaped port ambition, clearing work, Shreveport’s rise, and Grappe / Campti consequence.
