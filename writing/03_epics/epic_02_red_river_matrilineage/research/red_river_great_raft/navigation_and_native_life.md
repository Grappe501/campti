# Navigation and Native life (Great Raft context)

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Questions for research

- How did **local** river knowledge route trade, marriage visits, and seasonal movement when main channels shifted?

## Fiction-facing principle

**Knowledge as inheritance:** elders and providers read water the way they read weather — without explaining nineteenth-century commerce to the reader.
