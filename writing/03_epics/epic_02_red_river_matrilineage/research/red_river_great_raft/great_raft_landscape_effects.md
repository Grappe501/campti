# Great Raft — landscape effects

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Lived effects (fiction-facing)

- backed-up water, broad still reaches
- blocked side channels; dead timber; sediment bars
- seasonal change in what is passable
- fish habitat and fishing logic
- travel delays; portage or alternate routes
- “lake-like” quiet mistaken for permanence

## Rule

Describe **what bodies must do** in this water — not what **capital** will do later.
