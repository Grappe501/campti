# Campti port ambition

Canon status: OPEN / PROPOSED

## Human direction

The area of Campti was **promised or expected** to become a port (or otherwise **ascend** as a commercial hub). Family and local actors invested labor and hope in that geographic future.

Henry Shreve’s clearing of the Great Raft shifted **port power** north toward what became **Shreveport**. That shift became a **later** breakdown or consequence for families tied to Campti’s expected rise — including the Grappe line in later books.
