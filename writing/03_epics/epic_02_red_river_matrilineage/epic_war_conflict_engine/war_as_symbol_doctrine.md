# War as symbol doctrine

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

War and conflict should appear in every book.

Even foreign wars may be used if they affect the family, economy, records, politics, sons, husbands, wives, land, or public identity.

War itself should become a symbol.

## War symbolizes

- history forcing private families into public crisis
- men’s bodies being claimed by systems
- women holding continuity while systems fracture
- land changing hands
- records changing meaning
- children inheriting wounds they did not choose
- public duty versus family survival
- violence outside the home entering the home

## Rule

War should not become empty background.

Each war must touch:

- a household
- a marriage
- a body
- a record
- a field
- a child
- a woman’s decision
- a family transfer
