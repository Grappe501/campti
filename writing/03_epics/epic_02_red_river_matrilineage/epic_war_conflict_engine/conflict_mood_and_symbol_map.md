# Conflict mood and symbol map

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Pair **conflict type** with **mood + symbol** so set-pieces stay literary, not documentary.

| Conflict mood | Pace type (see `../language_doctrine/chapter_pace_design.md`) | Symbol companions | Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| siege of nerves (pre-battle) | binding + trade | cord, visitor object, silence | speechifying |
| sudden rupture | raid pace | sound, child body, river | gore porn |
| long grind | work + aftermath | food, farm, gray | numb montage |
