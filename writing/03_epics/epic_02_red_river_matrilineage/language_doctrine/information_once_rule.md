# Information Once Rule

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Rule

Important information should be placed with care, not repeated for emphasis.

If a detail matters, make its first appearance vivid, specific, and emotionally situated.

Then trust the reader to remember.

## Exceptions

A symbol may recur, but each recurrence must change meaning.

A fact may return only if:

- a new character interprets it differently
- the meaning has changed
- the cost has increased
- the reader now understands what they missed before
