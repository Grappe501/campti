# Beauty, Flow, Breath

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Protect **musicality** without ornament: long sentences when the river demands it; short strikes when conflict demands it.

## Rules

- **Flow follows physics:** breath matches exertion, heat, fear.
- **Beauty is earned** through precision, not adjective load.
- **Rhythm change** signals power shift — use deliberately, not constantly.
