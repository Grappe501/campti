# Poetic Historical Voice

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Desired effect

The prose should feel like a historian’s precision carried inside poetic language.

It should present encyclopedia-level fact through a beautiful storyline underneath it.

The voice should be detailed, flowing, serious, unapologetic, and emotionally alive.

## The voice should be

- factual without being dry
- beautiful without being ornamental
- poetic without becoming vague
- detailed without becoming a lecture
- emotionally forceful without melodrama
- historically dense without apology

## Rule

The facts are the bones.

The story is the blood.

The language is the breath.
