# Restraint and Reader Trust

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

Do not oversell details.

Readers should earn depth.

Do not make everything obvious.

Do not give the story away.

Do not repeat subjects or phrases just to make them sink in.

Give information once with enough force and placement that the reader retains it.

## Rule

Trust the reader.

Let symbols return quietly.

Let consequences reveal meaning later.

## Avoid

- explaining the metaphor
- repeating the same symbol too often
- telling the reader what to feel
- obvious foreshadowing
- thesis paragraphs
- repeated key phrases used as instructions
