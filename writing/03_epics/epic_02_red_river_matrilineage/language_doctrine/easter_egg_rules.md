# Easter Egg Rules

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Optional rewards for attentive readers — **never** required for comprehension.

## Rules

- Easter eggs must be **small** and **true to the moment’s realism**.
- Do not hide plot-critical information only in eggs.
- Avoid in-jokes that break historical frame unless deliberately framed.
