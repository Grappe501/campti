# False Ending Design

Canon status: PROPOSED

## The Ending the Reader Expects

- **PROPOSED (SRC-DUAL-001):** A **completed binding** (or binding threshold), communal blessing, sense of **“we will go on like this”** — continuity visible in faces, food, dance/labor songs, kin obligations renewed.

## Why That Ending Feels Earned

- **PROPOSED:** Because Acts I–III invested in **competence, obligation, humor, and sensory specificity** (`SRC-DUAL-003`, `SRC-DUAL-004`) — not because of twist mechanics.
- **PROPOSED (SRC-DUAL-005):** The matriarch/daughter relationship has carried real cost and real tenderness; binding rewards that work with **public joy**.

## Emotional Promises Made Before the Raid

- **PROPOSED:** “Your people know how to live.” “The river world holds.” “The daughter has a place.” “New kin ties will widen protection and meaning.”
- **OPEN:** Which promises are **explicitly spoken** vs shown — must avoid speechifying.

## Binding / Wedding Setup

- **PROPOSED:** Prep arcs: labor, gift logic, space-making, argument-makeups, dress/body modesty choices, elders’ approvals — **ordinary sacred hustle**.
- **OPEN:** Ceremony structure — blocked on ritual verification (`SRC-SPINE-001` gap).

## Community Gathering Function

- **PROPOSED:** Gathering concentrates **ensemble attachment** so loss is distributed grief, not only dyad spectacle.
- **PROPOSED:** Witness rules (`SRC-SPINE-001` constraint label) make the gathering **public truth** for the community — which the raid then shatters.

## Where Joy Peaks

- **PROPOSED:** Short window where **relief + pride** crest — reader exhales into belief: “this is the emotional landing.”
- **OPEN:** How long joy can run in Act IV before pacing sags — manifest question, not prose here.

## Where the Raid Breaks the Expected Shape

- **CONFIRMED (HI-001 + HI-002):** Expected shape = binding/continuity resolution; raid replaces it with **child-view collapse**, not battle clarity.
- **PROPOSED:** Break should feel like **interruption of a breath** — wrong sound first, not title card “ATTACK.”

## What Must Not Be Explained

- **CONFIRMED (HI-001 / HI-002):** Strategic why-now, clean military frame, political verdict, aftermath accounting, why spared — see `terminal_raid_knowledge_firewall.md`.

## Cut-to-Black Ending Image

A French soldier carries the daughter away from the destroyed encampment.

## Open Questions

- **OPEN:** Does any character **partially** foresee violence without transmitting thriller energy to reader — high risk; default **no**.

## Apparent Final Movement

- **PROPOSED:** Act IV reads like **last movement toward union + feast + witness** — camera weight on hands, voices, shared tasks.

## Wedding / Binding Momentum

- **PROPOSED:** Rising momentum through **prep completion**, arrival of distant kin, resolution of petty obstacles — all **life-forward**.

## Joy Before Rupture

- **PROPOSED:** Allocate explicit **joy beats** (games, teasing, food peak, elder humor) immediately pre-rupture so heartbreak is earned, not cruel.

## What the Reader Thinks Will Be Resolved

- **PROPOSED:** Kin tension tied to binding will **clear**; daughter’s small anxieties will **ease**; community will **enter a stable season**.

## What the Raid Actually Breaks

- **PROPOSED:** Continuity of **place + faces + soundscape**; assumption that adults can always interpose; assumption that ceremony marks safety.

## Last Safe Sequence Before Collapse

- **PROPOSED:** Final “safe” = a **small daughter-scale victory** (she is seen, she contributes, she is proud) — then rupture.
- **OPEN:** Define “safe” without telegraphing doom — audit against no-thriller rules.

## Cut Point Discipline

- **CONFIRMED (HI-001):** End at carry-away; **no** next-chapter preview; **no** narrator reassurance.
- **PROPOSED:** Any epigraph or chapter tag devices — default **off** near close to avoid omniscience creep.
