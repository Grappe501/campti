# Terminal Raid Knowledge Firewall

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Prevent the final sequence from becoming exposition, strategy, or adult historical explanation.

## Final POV Rule

The raid is experienced from the daughter’s viewpoint.

## Reader Knowledge Limit

The reader cannot know more than the daughter can emotionally process in the moment.

## Allowed in Final Sequence

- sensory terror
- confusion
- broken glimpses
- known faces vanishing
- sound, smoke, bodies, movement
- the impossible fact of being spared
- being lifted and carried away

## Not Allowed in Final Sequence

- explanation of why the raid is happening
- full military context
- adult political summary
- omniscient historical framing
- aftermath explanation
- clean understanding by the daughter

## Final Image

The daughter is carried away by a French soldier.

## Cut Point

End before explanation.
