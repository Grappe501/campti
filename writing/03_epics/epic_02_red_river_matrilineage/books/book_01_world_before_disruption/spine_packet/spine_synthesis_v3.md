## Integrated spine thesis (HI-001 + HI-002)

Book 01 **appears** to be moving toward a **wedding/binding** and **communal continuity**. The reader is meant to **fall in love with the people** and believe the ending will **affirm life continuing**. The **final raid** destroys that expectation and is experienced through the **terrified, limited viewpoint** of the **matriarch’s daughter** — sudden, incomprehensible, **not** a battle report; the closing image remains **a French soldier carrying her away** from the destroyed encampment, with **no** strategic explanation or aftermath summary on-page.

## Dual-Track Spine

Book 01 now runs on a visible wedding/binding track and a hidden historical-pressure track.

The visible track should dominate the reader’s emotional experience.

The hidden track should remain mostly invisible until the ending, except for details that create retroactive coherence.

The reader should not feel they are waiting for a massacre. The reader should feel they are waiting for a binding, continuity, and joy.

## 1. Opening condition

- **PROPOSED:** Book 1 opens on a **working river-and-hamlet civilization** with visible seasonal obligation rhythms, **matrilineal continuity work** at the center, and **layered stresses** already measurable (environment, food security, kin reciprocity, outside unknowns).
- **Anchors:** `SRC-SPINE-003`, `SRC-SPINE-001`, `SRC-SPINE-002`, `SRC-SPINE-006`.
- **HI-002 note:** early/mid book may carry **small tensions, distant unease, misunderstood signs** — **retroactive-coherence foreshadowing only**; **not** thriller anticipation of massacre.

## 2. Disruption event

- **PROPOSED (HI-001 + HI-002 + D-003):** **Hidden pressure / historical instability** beneath a **visible joy runway** (wedding/binding, community attachment) → **sudden catastrophic shock** at the **ending French raid** on the Natchitoches encampment.
- **PROPOSED:** Mid-book shocks (if any) remain **sub-threshold** unless separately chosen; the **defining irreversible break** is the raid sequence; reader should **not** feel they were **waiting for the attack**.

## 3. Emotional center

- **PROPOSED:** Matrilineal continuity remains core (`SRC-SPINE-002`).
- **PROPOSED (HI-001 + HI-002 + D-004):** **Matriarch / daughter** axis remains load-bearing; **ensemble/community attachment** is equally load-bearing so the rupture lands as **loss**; **daughter** is the **final viewpoint vessel** for the attack; **daughter must be emotionally legible** before the ending; final sequence is **daughter-limited** comprehension (POV mode still **OPEN**).

## 4. Ending condition
