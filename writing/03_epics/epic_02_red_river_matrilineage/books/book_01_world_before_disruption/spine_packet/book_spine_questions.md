## 1 — What is the world/state at the opening?

**OPEN prompts:**

- What is the world *before* disruption (one paragraph max, PROPOSED)?
- What does daily life look like for the **emotional center** character(s)?
- What systems are **stable** on the surface?
- What is **already unstable** beneath the surface?
- What does the reader need to **feel** is at stake *before* anything breaks?

**Links:** `opening_condition.md`

---

## 2 — What breaks that world?

**OPEN prompts:**

- What is the **disruption event** (or first irreversible pressure), structurally?
- Is disruption external, internal, environmental, social — or braided?
- What **cannot** go back to “before” after this break?

**Links:** `disruption_event.md`

---

## 3 — Who carries the emotional center?

**OPEN prompts:**

- Whose want / fear / obligation drives the reader’s pulse?
- Who is the **memory carrier** (if applicable to this project’s laws)?
- Who is mistaken, partial, or powerful — and how does that braid with theme?

**Links:** `emotional_center.md`

---

## 4 — What is transformed by the end of Book 1?

**OPEN prompts:**

- What **world knowledge** does the reader have at the end that they lacked at the opening?
- What **relationship or power map** has shifted?
- What **doorway** opens to the larger epic (without resolving it)?

**Links:** `ending_condition.md`, `act_structure.md`
