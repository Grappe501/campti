## Core Storyline

Book 01’s core storyline is **not** the raid.

The core storyline is a Native community **led by a powerful opening matriarch** as **three generations of women** prepare for a wedding/binding that appears to secure continuity across families and kinship. Through the matriarch’s household, the **granddaughter’s** child-world, and the women who hold the village together, the reader falls in love with the people, children, households, humor, trade life, and future being built.

The hidden Red River pressure remains mostly beneath the surface.

The raid breaks the story the reader thought they were reading — destroying the **granddaughter’s** expected future and carrying **her** away (HI-001 + HI-004).

**Narrative center:** women’s power, memory, labor, leadership, and survival; men may dominate records but must not displace this center (`core_storyline/`, `HI-004`).
