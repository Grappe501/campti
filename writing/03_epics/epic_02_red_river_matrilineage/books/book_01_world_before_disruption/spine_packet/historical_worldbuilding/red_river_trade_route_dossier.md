# Red River Trade Route Dossier

Canon status: OPEN / PROPOSED

## Purpose

Explain the Red River trade world that shaped Natchitoches, Native communities, French movement, Spanish pressure, exchange, diplomacy, and family formation.

## Questions To Answer

- What was moving along the Red River?
- Who controlled or mediated trade?
- Which Native communities were involved?
- What did French traders want?
- What did Native communities want or resist?
- How did trade alter relationships, marriages, alliances, and danger?
- What would children notice about trade?
- What would women control, observe, prepare, exchange, or interpret?
- What objects should appear in village life?
- What should not appear because it is wrong for the time/place?

## Story Use

The trade route should not be a lecture.

It should appear through:

- arrivals
- goods
- gossip
- obligations
- marriages
- warnings
- gifts
- debts
- visitors
- seasonal movement
- children watching adults
