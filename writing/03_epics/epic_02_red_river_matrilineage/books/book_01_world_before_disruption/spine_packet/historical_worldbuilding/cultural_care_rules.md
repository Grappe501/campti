# Cultural Care Rules

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Core Rules

- Do not genericize Native life.
- Do not merge Chitimacha, Natchitoches, Caddo, or other Native identities without evidence.
- Do not use ceremony as decoration.
- Do not invent sacred material casually.
- Do not make Native characters exist only to suffer.
- Do not make the village idyllic in a childish way; make it alive, complex, funny, loving, political, and human.
- Do not explain everything through French eyes.
- Do not let the raid define the whole people.
- Build life first. Rupture second.

## Manuscript Rule

The book should teach through lived experience, not lectures.
