# Central Question

Canon status: PROPOSED

**HI-004:** Questions apply across **matriarch → mother generation → granddaughter**; terminal child POV remains **granddaughter**. **HI-005:** Book structured in **three thirds** aligned to those generations. **HI-006:** Series continuation after Book 01 ending — **PROPOSED** / OPEN until sourced.

## Surface Question (HI-005)

Will the granddaughter’s wedding/binding secure the future her mother and grandmother built?

## Deeper Question (HI-005)

Can matriarchal power carry a people forward when history is moving toward rupture?

## Granddaughter Question

What does a child understand of belonging before belonging is violently taken?

## Epic Question (HI-005)

What survives across centuries when women’s lives carry the line that men’s records only partly name?

## Series question (HI-006 — PROPOSED)

How does matriarchal power **survive and adapt** across captivity, marriage, widowhood, trade, and colonial systems so the line — and women’s work within it — remains historically legible across Books 02–05?

## Matriarch Question (HI-004 — OPEN)

What does leadership cost when the future you built for the next generations is destroyed in a single rupture?
