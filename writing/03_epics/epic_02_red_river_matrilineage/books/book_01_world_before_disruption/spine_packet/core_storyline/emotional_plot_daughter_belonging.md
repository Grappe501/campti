**Filename note:** Legacy path `emotional_plot_daughter_belonging.md` — content tracks the **granddaughter / young heroine** per **HI-004**. Use **daughter** only for explicit **mother–daughter** (middle generation) bonds.

## Purpose

The **granddaughter** must be a full child before she becomes the survivor.

The raid happens to **her** known universe as **matriarch’s granddaughter**, not to an unnamed “daughter” of the community in the abstract.

## Emotional Plot Question

What does belonging mean to her before the world is taken?
