# Village Ensemble Story Engine

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

The novel needs **epic village stories**, not only plot events: enough named relationships that the reader **loves the people** before rupture.

**HI-004:** Ensemble work serves **women’s lived world** — matriarchal leadership, households, labor, memory, binding prep — while men (traders, soldiers, kin) appear as history requires **without** displacing narrative center.

## Functions

- Distribute page-time across **households** and **generations** (`../historical_worldbuilding/family_life_matrix.md`, `relationship_web.md`, `child_cohort_map.md`).
- Tie each cluster to **visible plot** (binding) and **hidden plot** (Red River pressure) without thriller wiring.
- Ensure **opening matriarch** has enough early presence that her authority and cost register before the raid.
- Keep **granddaughter** in a **child cohort**, not alone.
