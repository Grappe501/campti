## Purpose

The visible plot gives the reader a reason to turn pages before the raid.

The reader should believe the book is moving toward union, continuity, family future, and joy — and that the **granddaughter’s** future is the **people’s** future (**HI-005**).

## Visible Plot Question

Will the binding come together, and what future will it create?

**HI-004 / HI-005:** Will **intergenerational** matriarchal work and this binding secure Red River leadership and balance?

## Plot Functions

- gather families
- reveal relationships
- introduce children (**granddaughter’s** cohort and wider child-world)
- create stakes without thriller tension
- show village life
- show trade route influence through arrivals/goods/obligations
- let reader fall in love with the community
- create the expected ending that the raid breaks
- **establish opening matriarch as community lead** (**Third I**)
- **show mother generation transforming power** through binding, including **Campti (PROPOSED)** thread (**Third II**)
- **Third III:** convergence on **granddaughter’s** binding promise then rupture
