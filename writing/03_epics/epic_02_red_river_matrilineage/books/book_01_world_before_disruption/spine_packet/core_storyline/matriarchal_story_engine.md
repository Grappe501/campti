## Governing Principle

The novel is centered on women’s power, memory, labor, kinship, leadership, and survival.

Men may dominate many historical records, but the story should reveal the world those records often miss.

**HI-005:** Book is broken into **three major thirds**; each third focuses on a woman **becoming, holding, or transmitting** matriarchal power — map scaffold to **Third I / II / III** in `three_thirds_matriarchal_structure.md`.
