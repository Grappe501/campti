# Hidden Plot — Red River Pressure

Canon status: PROPOSED / OPEN pending verification

## Purpose

The hidden plot gives retroactive coherence to the ending without turning the book into a raid countdown.

## Hidden Plot Question

What is changing around the village that the child and reader cannot fully interpret yet?

**HI-004 (parallel):** The Red River world is changing in ways even strong matriarchal leadership cannot fully control.

## Allowed Pressures

- trade route shifts
- French movement
- goods arriving differently
- rumors
- adult caution
- obligations
- diplomacy
- visitors
- distant violence mentioned indirectly
- tensions that appear ordinary before the ending

## Forbidden Handling

- no villain-forward suspense
- no obvious raid countdown
- no repeated warnings
- no external narrator telling the reader doom is coming
- no scenes whose only purpose is foreshadowing violence

## Retroactive Rule

After the ending, earlier details should feel newly meaningful.

Before the ending, they should feel like part of a living world.
