## Purpose

Book 01 is structured around three generations of women and the transfer, strengthening, and threatened rupture of matriarchal power.

## Third III — Granddaughter / Young Heroine

### Story Function

Show the most promising future of the line.

The granddaughter is the child of leadership and the focus of the wedding/binding false ending.

She represents continuity, alliance, future matriarchal power, and the promise that the Red River leadership structure will endure.

### Core Question

Will the granddaughter’s binding carry the people’s future forward?

### Power She Is Meant To Carry

- matriarchal inheritance
- leadership bloodline
- intertribal kinship balance
- future household authority
- peace-binding promise
- Grappe-line continuity

### Raid Function

The raid destroys the expected future at the moment the reader believes continuity may win.

The granddaughter is carried away.

**HI-006 (series / structure):** The raid breaks the **visible** Book 01 future but does **not** end the line or matriarchal power in the epic; power **adapts** across later arenas (`../series_arc/`, `../human_inputs/HI-006_*.md`). The **reader** is not given the full sequel map at the close.
