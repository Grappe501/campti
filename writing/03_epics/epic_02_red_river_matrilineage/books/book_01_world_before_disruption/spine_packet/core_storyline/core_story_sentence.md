## Series power continuity (HI-006)

The raid breaks the **visible** future in Book 01 but does **not** end the line or the power. It forces matriarchal power to **adapt** into French colonial, marriage, trade, land, and Red River systems across later books. Book 01 still **ends** at carry-away (**HI-001**); the **reader** is not owed the full sequel explanation at the closing beat — the **spine** must track that she is **not erased** (`series_arc/`, `spine_synthesis_v8.md`).

Book 01 follows three generations of Native women as matriarchal power is built, strengthened, and prepared for transmission through a granddaughter’s wedding/binding. The opening matriarch leads the community, her daughter binds the family into Red River leadership through Campti, and the granddaughter appears poised to carry the future forward before a historical raid destroys the expected continuity and carries her into the next lineage of the epic.

**Note:** “Through Campti” = **King Campti / Campti marriage thread** — **PROPOSED**, intentional fictionalized history **pending verification**; not `01_canon/` fact until sourced (**HI-005**).

## Story Engine (HI-004 + HI-005)

- **Visible story (not “the raid is coming”):** Will **intergenerational** matriarchal work and the **granddaughter’s** binding secure Red River leadership, peace, and kinship balance?
- **Hidden story:** Colonial Red River world changing beneath ordinary life — not fully controllable even by strong matriarchal leadership.
- **Ending rupture (Third III):** Raid destroys the expected future; **granddaughter** carried away (HI-001).
