# Book spine packet — `book_01_world_before_disruption`

**Canon status:** PROPOSED (whole packet) unless a line explicitly cites a **CONFIRMED** `writing/01_canon/` pointer.

## Purpose

Structured prompts to answer **five spine questions** before any chapter folders or draft prose:

1. What is the world/state at the opening?
2. What breaks that world?
3. Who carries the emotional center?
4. What is transformed by the end of Book 1?
5. What question does Book 1 answer, and what larger question does it leave open?
