## Native Worldbuilding Mandate

The story must build a historically grounded Native world across three generations.

The reader should become deeply invested in village life, family systems, children, trade, binding, humor, work, love, and future continuity.

The Red River trade route should be treated as a living network shaping arrival, exchange, kinship, risk, diplomacy, and pressure.

The raid is not the point of the people’s lives. It is the rupture of lives already made real.
