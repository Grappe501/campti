## 1. Governing Structure

- Book 01 is built in **three major thirds**, each foregrounding a generation of women and **matriarchal power**: **becoming / holding / transmitting** authority, permanence, and intertribal balance (**HI-005**).
- Narrative center remains **women’s POV and lived experience**; men (chiefs, traders, warriors, husbands, fathers — including the **granddaughter’s father** as tribe male leader) may be important but do not replace structural center (**HI-004** + **HI-005**).
- **King Campti / mother’s marriage:** **PROPOSED** — intentional fictionalized-history stretch — **not** verified fact; track separately from `01_canon/` until sourced (**HI-005**).

## 2. Third I — Opening Matriarch

- **Story function:** Establish woman-led world: community authority, memory, kinship, trade/danger literacy, household power, peace-binding logic.
- **Core question (HI-005):** How does the opening matriarch hold the world together?
- **Power:** Pattern for women’s leadership; what she passes forward = **TBD** in `three_thirds_matriarchal_structure.md`.
- **Cross-links:** `matriarchal_story_engine.md`, `historical_worldbuilding/village_life_system.md`, attachment maps.

## 3. Third II — Daughter / Mother Generation

- **Story function:** Next matriarchal figure **receives and transforms** power; marriage/binding, alliance, household leadership, motherhood, political intelligence; **prepares the granddaughter**.
- **Core question:** How does the daughter turn inherited authority into lasting family power?
- **King Campti thread:** mother’s marriage — **PROPOSED** fictionalized history; verify basis (Le Roi Campti / Chief Campti / manuscript vs internal-only naming) before any canon claim.
- **Pattern:** prior-generation women also bound to **heirs/leaders of other tribal groups** for peace and balance (**HI-005** — verify plausibility and terminology).

## 4. Third III — Granddaughter / Young Heroine

- **Story function:** “Future of the line” and leadership bloodline; **wedding/binding false ending** (HI-002); reader believes continuity of Red River leadership may endure.
- **Core question:** Will the granddaughter’s binding carry the people’s future forward?
- **Raid (HI-001 + HI-004 + HI-005):** Occurs in **this third**; destroys expected future; **granddaughter** carried away; terminal child-limited POV unchanged.
- **Story-world role:** Pledged binding to tribe’s **strongest male leader** candidate — reframe options OPEN (political strength, ritual fitness, kinship designation — **HI-005** verification); avoid default European “princess / king” unless deliberate outsider frame.
