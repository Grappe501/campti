## HI-002 Refinement

The emotional center must expand beyond the matriarch/daughter axis into community attachment.

The daughter remains the final viewpoint vessel, but the book must make the reader love the people around her first.

The wedding/binding should serve as an emotional gathering point for:

- matriarchal continuity
- family relationships
- community humor and tenderness
- future promise
- cultural life
- reader attachment

The final raid only works if the reader fears for characters they have come to love.

## The Question

Who is Book 1’s **emotional center**, and what is the **POV / generational scope**: two-hand matriarchal spine vs wider three-generation presence?

## Why This Matters

This choice sets **POV workload**, **interiority rules** (`SRC-SPINE-002`), and whether `chunk9` scaffolding is **on-stage** or **research runway**.

## Option A

### Description

**Two-hand Book 1:** primary emotional center is **two matrilineal carriers** (mother/daughter functions). Third generation exists only as **off-page pressure** (rumor, belly, elder talk) unless Book 1 explicitly expands later.

### Strengths

Clearest match to `chunk8` spine; manageable POV discipline.

### Risks

May underuse `chunk9` generational richness if that corpus is important to stakeholders.

### What this option makes possible

Tight relationship novel inside a system novel.

### What this option blocks or delays

Full triangulation of three adult POVs in one volume.

## Option B

### Description

**Three-generation ensemble:** three explicit POV carriers across generations (rotating limited).

### Strengths

Maximal use of census scaffolding and macro-demographic emotion.

### Risks

Higher craft burden; can dilute matrilineal “carrier” clarity if not disciplined.

### What this option makes possible

A wider demographic fresco in one book.

### What this option blocks or delays

Fast reader bonding with a narrow dyad.

## Option C, if needed

### Description

**Two-hand on-page + third generation as limited interludes** (letters, council witness, non-POV chapters) — **not** co-equal POV unless expanded later.

### Strengths

Compromise path: richness without full tri-POV.

### Risks

Interludes can feel like cheating if not clearly bounded.

### What this option makes possible

Foreshadowing epic runway while keeping center dyad.

### What this option blocks or delays

True tri-POV symmetry.

## Recommended Provisional Choice

**Option A — Two-hand spine (HI-001 constrained):** mother-function / daughter-function remain central; **daughter legibility** is now a **hard structural requirement** leading into the terminal sequence.

## Reason for Recommendation

**HI-001** makes the daughter’s prior interior life and relationship context load-bearing for ethical and reader-trust reasons (avoid spectacle).
