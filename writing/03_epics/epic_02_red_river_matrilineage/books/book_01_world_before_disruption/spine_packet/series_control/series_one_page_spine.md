## Core Epic Sentence

Across nine books, the Grappe / Campti / Red River line moves from Native matriarchal power and trade-route kinship through colonial rupture, French adaptation, Red River brokerage, Civil War, Reconstruction, Depression-era Campti, and modern Arkansas public life — with women carrying the line’s continuity even when men become the visible historical markers.
