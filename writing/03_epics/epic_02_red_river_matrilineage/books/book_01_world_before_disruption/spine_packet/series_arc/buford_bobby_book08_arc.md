# Book 08 — Buford Isadore Grappe / Bobby-Barbara Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED pending documentation

## Purpose

Book 08 centers Buford Isadore Grappe and his wife Bobby / Barbara as Depression-era and 20th-century Campti family builders.

## Working Human Direction

Buford Isadore Grappe:

- lived through the Depression
- served as a one-armed city marshal
- lost one arm at age 18 in a hunting accident
- was shot again in his 40s while turkey hunting
- raised 13 surviving children with Bobby / Barbara
- helped reshape Campti around the family persona

Bobby / Barbara:

- wife of Buford
- mother of 13 surviving children
- household and continuity center
- must be researched and developed as a central woman, not background

## Story Function

Buford gives the book a public persona.

Bobby / Barbara gives the book its continuity engine.

## Research Questions

- Buford’s full dates
- Bobby / Barbara’s full name and dates
- marriage date
- names of the 13 surviving children
- confirmation of Buford’s marshal role
- confirmation of the two hunting incidents
- how Campti changed around this family
- what Bobby / Barbara’s labor, authority, grief, humor, and strength looked like
