## Arena Shift

| Phase | Where power is exercised | Primary levers | Status |
|-------|---------------------------|----------------|--------|
| Book 01 pre-raid | Native community / binding / kinship | matriarchal leadership, intertribal balance | PROPOSED |
| Rupture | raid / captivity / displacement | violence, French military | HI-001 |
| Post-raid adaptation | French colonial institutions + household | marriage, records, patronage, language | **HI-006** PROPOSED |
| Trade stronghold | fort / Red River / Caddo / north bend | brokerage, land, kinship across systems | **HI-006** PROPOSED |

## Rule

Power **continues**; its **arena** and **visible form** change. Do not narrate Book 01 as “the end of power.”
