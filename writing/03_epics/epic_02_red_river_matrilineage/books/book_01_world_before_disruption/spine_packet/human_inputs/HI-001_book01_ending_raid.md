# HI-001 — Book 01 Ending Raid

**Decision status:** HUMAN SELECTED  
**Canon status:** PROPOSED pending canon promotion  
**Related decisions:** D-003, D-004, D-005  
**Date recorded:** 2026-05-12

## Human Direction Summary

Book 01 ends with a French raid on the Natchitoches encampment.

The raid destroys the encampment. Men, women, and children are killed.

The daughter of the matriarch, approximately 11 or 12 years old and small-framed, is spared.

The ending is experienced through her terror and confusion. It feels as if the world is literally collapsing around her while she remains physically untouched.

She does not understand what is happening.

A French soldier grabs her, puts her over his shoulder, and carries her away from the battlefield.

That is all the reader knows at the end.

## Important Story Rules

- Do not explain the full political or historical context in the final moment.
- Do not explain why she is spared unless that has been carefully seeded earlier.
- Do not show aftermath beyond the soldier carrying her away.
- Keep the ending inside the **granddaughter’s** fear, confusion, and sensory experience (**HI-004** kinship label; HI-001 unchanged outcome).
- The ending should feel like world-collapse, not a battle report.
- This is an ending condition, not a prose draft.

## Refinement

See `HI-002_wedding_binding_false_ending.md`.

The raid should not be framed as the expected ending. The visible emotional trajectory of Book 01 should appear to be a wedding or binding, continuity, and communal joy. The raid should rupture that expectation.

### HI-004 — Matriarchal three-generation engine (2026-05-12)

Spine documents refine the focal child as the **granddaughter** of the **opening matriarch** (community lead). HI-001’s original phrase “daughter of the matriarch” remains above as the recorded snapshot; use **granddaughter** / **young heroine** in planning unless a verified kin chart makes **daughter** literally correct. Raid outcome unchanged: spared without understanding; **carried away**.

### HI-005 — Three-thirds structure (2026-05-12)

Book 01 is structured in **three major thirds**; the **raid occurs in the third third** (granddaughter section). See `HI-005_three_thirds_matriarchal_ascension_structure.md`. **King Campti** material is **PROPOSED** fictionalized history — not verified fact for ending framing.

### HI-006 — Post-raid power continuity (2026-05-12)

The **epic** continues matriarchal power after the raid through adaptation (Jacques Guedon, French-system fluency, Marie Louise Besson/Grappe marriages, trade stronghold, Francois as later carrier). **Book 01 ending unchanged** — reader still stops at carry-away. See `HI-006_post_raid_power_continuity_grappe_arc.md`, `series_arc/`, `spine_synthesis_v8.md`. Claims **PROPOSED** until documented.
