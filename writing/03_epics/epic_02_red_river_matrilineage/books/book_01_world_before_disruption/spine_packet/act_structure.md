### Act IV — Binding / Collapse

**Visible purpose:**  
The book appears to move toward wedding/binding, celebration, continuity, and future promise.

**Hidden structural purpose:**  
The binding gathers the community emotionally and physically so the reader feels the full cost of the rupture.

**Terminal rupture:**

**Knowledge rule:**  
The reader should not know more than the daughter can emotionally process once the raid begins.
