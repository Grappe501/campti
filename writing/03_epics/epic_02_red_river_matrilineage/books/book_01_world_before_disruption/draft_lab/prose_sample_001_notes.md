# Prose Sample 001 Notes

## What worked

- **River-as-workplace opening:** calf-deep stance, current at ankles, mist/pearl and mud/smoke taste land immediately in **body + place** without naming a colonizer frame first.
- **Sound map of women’s labor:** “scrape of blades on wood,” pottery tested for cracks, fish on stone with scales catching light—each sound **does work** for world scale and competence culture.
- **Power through orientation, not speech:** hearths redirect smoke “in practiced directions”; people use tasks instead of speeches on the long walk; “eyes answered in small units” shows authority as **exchange**, not decree.
- **Copper beat:** “thin and honest” smell + “making people hurry” ties **material culture to tempo and power** without naming a system lecture.
- **Teaching body:** “showed how the knot could breathe” / “The girl breathed with it” is a **clean matriarchal transfer**—matches I-A scene candidate for net/knot skill.
- **Binding thread without ceremony dump:** shawl folded, non-local fiber, “when she is grown enough to carry a day”—**future continuity in cloth** lands as lived strategy, not glossary.
- **Humor and warmth:** kettle / fire line; dogs and bone—**ensemble life** so the village is not only solemn dignity.

## What felt too thin

- **Named relationships:** “Aunt” and “one of her daughter's children” help, but the daughter-mother generation is **heard, not shown**; no second adult woman in the matriarch’s own cohort on stage with distinct voice.
- **Specific ecology:** “finfish,” “blueblack… farther water” gestures at trade fish vs local but **species and season** stay generic; river could use one or two **place-locked** anchors (plant names, bird calls beyond heron) once research allows.
- **Trade pressure as social body:** strangers path is mentioned; **no visitor body** appears—pressure stays abstract compared to I-A’s optional visitor scene.
- **“Girl with the freckles”:** quick humanizing tag but **lightly generic**; freckles as ID may read modern/universal unless grounded in one remembered trait the village uses.

## What felt too modern

- **Psychological vocabulary:** “the part of her that recorded futures” reads like **contemporary interior framing**; same for “small units of yes, not yet, wait, I know” (clever but essay-adjacent).
- **A few compressed aphorisms:** “Continuities lived in cloth as much as in blood” is **true thematically** but slightly **narrator-explanatory** compared to the more embodied beats around it.
- **“Human roar”** — not wrong, but tonally close to modern idiom; worth checking against preferred diction pass.

## What felt culturally risky

- **Guest protocol for unknown fish / “watch it”:** ethically careful on the page, but **real communities** had specific rules about **foreign food, contamination, obligation**—must not imply a invented “noble protocol” without sources; flag for **ethnography + safety realism** (illness, taboo, gift exchange).
- **Mother’s river line** (“relative that forgot your name”): beautiful and non-European-centered, but **spiritual framing** can slide into **pan-Indigenous proverb voice** if it isn’t tied to **this** family’s actual teaching idiom—needs human review for tone and accuracy.
- **Copper:** historically loaded; **who brought it, on what terms** matters for **power truth**—right now it is atmospheric; avoid accidental **timeless copper mystique** without record discipline.

## What needs research

- **Chitimacha / anchor nation:** material life, river tasks, net cordage, pottery, food species, seasonality—per `spine_packet/historical_worldbuilding/` and HI-003/HI-004 queues.
- **Trade goods profile:** what would plausibly arrive as **wrapped different** without turning into **European inventory theater**.
- **Binding / cloth prep:** culturally accurate **preparation practices** (who ties, who dyes, what witnesses mean)—`binding_plot_manifest.md` + verification pass before locking lines about “witnesses.”
- **Household spatial terms:** “three hearths,” visitor-wait place—layout plausibility vs **generic village diagram**.

- **Fish-as-guest** caution (after research confirmation / adjustment).
