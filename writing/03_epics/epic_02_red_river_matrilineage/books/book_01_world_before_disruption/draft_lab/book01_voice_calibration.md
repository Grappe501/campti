# Book 01 Voice Calibration

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Desired Voice

Huge, immersive, historically grounded, women-centered, nature-rich, emotionally intimate, and generational.

## Voice Should Feel

- ancient but not archaic
- lush but not purple
- detailed but not encyclopedic
- intimate but not small
- historically dense but not lecture-heavy
- emotionally powerful without melodrama
- Native-centered without genericizing
- women-centered without flattening men

## Voice Should Avoid

- modern phrasing that breaks time/place
- generic “tribal” language
- museum-label exposition
- thriller foreshadowing
- trauma-first framing
- overexplaining matriarchal authority
- making the world feel doomed from the start

## Test Question

Does the prose make the reader want to live in this world before history breaks it?
