# Prose Sample 001 RevB Notes

## What changed from RevA

- **Authority lock:** early **smoke-read** + one-touch redirect (“Cover first”) reroutes a woman’s steps **before** a mistake becomes talk; **rack dispute** (sun vs shade) settled by **knuckle + pin shift** without argument-as-speech; **copper** beat paired with **dry elder exchange** (“teaches hurry” / “teach it back”).
- **Removed** maxim line “going first is also a kind of answer” → plain **“She went toward the racks.”**
- **Replaced** “when fear drips” with **mouth runs ahead** (still interpretive but **embodied habit** language).
- **Canon-softening:** dropped **cypress** species name → **“bark along the bend”** (still **PROPOSED** sensory—flag until flora verified).
- **Attachment:** **crooked-bundle teaching** (deliberate wrong left); **salt fish half** pressed into child’s palm **without** sentiment speech; **kettle girl** at sand’s edge **watching cord** closes on **child + work** not theme.
- **Ending:** cut “name without naming” / “lie forgiven” thesis-adjacent lines; close on **thumb on cord, child watching, mist, laughter swallowed by water**—image-led.

## Authority lock improvement

- **Three** embodied reads of power: **smoke/workflow correction**; **fish/pin** physical settlement; **copper** tempo teaching + elder volley.
- Men/traveler remain **peripheral**; authority interprets **trade signs** (wrong fish, copper hurry) through **procedure and hands**.

## Reader attachment improvement

- **Bundle stack** beat: competence + patience + **intentional imperfection** as pedagogy.
- **Fish half** in palm: tenderness **inside** labor norms.
- **Kettle girl** ending: imitation-as-love without naming love.

## Canon-safety improvement

- No ceremony names, no tribe label, no titles, no European monarchy diction.
- **Specific plant/tree name removed** (cypress out); **“blueblack”** fish remains **generic trade-stranger** marker—still needs **research** for real species / season language when ready.

## Voice improvement

- Less **essay** in closing; fewer **maxim** hinges; one line (“learning could happen through the eyes alone”) still **borderline**—flag for human ear (could cut to pure gesture).

## Remaining risks

- **Smoke correction** assumes village layout knowledge—**layout** still OPEN in research.
- **Fish half** gesture must stay **culturally plausible** under verification (not every community frames “work food” the same way).
