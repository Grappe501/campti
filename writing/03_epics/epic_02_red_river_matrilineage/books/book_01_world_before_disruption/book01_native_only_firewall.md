# Book 01 Native-only firewall

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Governing rule

Book 01 contains **no developed non-Native characters**.

No Alexis Grappe.  
No Jacques Guedon.  
No Jean Baptiste Besson.  
No French viewpoint.  
No French-centered plotline.  
No non-Native character development.

## Allowed

Non-Native presence may appear only as:

- distant rumor
- trade trace
- remembered disease
- adult caution
- unnamed soldier reference
- object / good moving through trade
- off-page pressure
- unexplained final raid force seen through the granddaughter’s terror

## Not allowed

- French POV
- Alexis introduction
- Guedon introduction
- developed soldier character
- French dialogue scenes
- French political explanation
- non-Native romance setup
- colonial exposition
- adult narrator explaining French motives

## Book 02 boundary

The full non-Native viewpoint and French-system story begins in **Book 02**.

Book 01 belongs to the **Native world**.
