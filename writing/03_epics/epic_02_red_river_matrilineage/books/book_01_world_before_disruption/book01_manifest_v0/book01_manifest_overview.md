# Book 01 Manifest Overview V0

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Governing Story

Three generations of Native women build a future through matriarchal power, binding, kinship, and Red River leadership. The granddaughter appears poised to inherit and extend that future through a wedding/binding before a historical raid destroys the expected continuity and carries her away.

## Structure

| Third | Focus Woman | Story Function | Power Built | Transfer / Ending |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Third I | Opening matriarch | Establish women-led world | memory, kinship, leadership, village order | power pattern passes to daughter |
| Third II | Daughter / mother generation | Strengthen permanence | marriage/binding, household power, intertribal alliance | future concentrates in granddaughter |
| Third III | Granddaughter / young heroine | Embody the promised future | binding promise, leadership inheritance, reader attachment | raid carries her away but line/power survive |

## Visible Story

The book appears to move toward wedding/binding, continuity, and joy.

## Hidden Story

The Red River world is changing in ways the characters and reader do not fully understand.

## Ending

The raid collapses the expected wedding/binding future from the granddaughter’s terrified viewpoint.

## What Survives

The line, the power, the memory, and the capacity to adapt.
