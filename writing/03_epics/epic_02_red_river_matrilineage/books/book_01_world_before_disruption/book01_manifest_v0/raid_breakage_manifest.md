## Purpose

Clarify what the raid breaks at the end of Book 01.

## The Raid Breaks

- the visible wedding/binding future
- the granddaughter’s expected role
- the matriarch’s planned continuity
- the mother’s secured future for her child
- the village’s assumption of tomorrow
- the reader’s expected ending

## The Raid Does Not Break

- the line
- the power
- the memory
- women’s continuity
- the epic’s forward motion

## Final Viewpoint Rule

The raid is experienced through the granddaughter’s terror and confusion.

## Final Cut Point

The granddaughter is carried away by a French soldier.

End before explanation.
