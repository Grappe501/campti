# Binding Plot Manifest

Canon status: PROPOSED / OPEN pending cultural verification

## Purpose

The binding is the visible plot engine of Book 01.

## Binding Story Question

Will the granddaughter’s binding secure the future her mother and grandmother built?

## Binding Functions

- family continuity
- intertribal peace
- Red River leadership balance
- matriarchal transfer
- reader expectation of joy
- false ending structure

## Binding Must Feel Like

- a real future
- a communal event
- a continuation of women’s strategy
- a reason for households to gather
- a source of humor, tension, pride, fear, and hope

## Not Allowed

- generic wedding fantasy
- culturally careless ceremony
- bait-only plot device
- European monarchy assumptions
- treating the granddaughter only as property

## Open Questions

- Who is the pledged husband?
- What is the culturally accurate form of binding?
- What does the granddaughter understand?
- What do her mother and grandmother intend?
- What does the binding secure politically, spiritually, socially, or economically?
