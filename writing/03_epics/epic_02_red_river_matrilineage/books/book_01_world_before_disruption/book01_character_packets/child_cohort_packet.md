# Character Packet — Child cohort (collective + named slots OPEN)

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Identity

Children and young adolescents who share paths, chores, insults, and imitation—not a single “symbol child.” Slots below are **typing tools**; names, exact ages, and kin labels **OPEN** until locked against research and Sample 002 disposition.

## Book Function

Populate **child-world** so the granddaughter’s POV is not solitary genius loneliness; carry **fairness, shame, comedy, and small cruelties** as moral weather. Makes the village **future** feel crowded and worth defending.

## Public Role

Runs messages; squabbles over sunny patches; repeats adult phrases wrong; tests edges of women’s quiet; throws pebbles; listens for laughter that is **not quite** ridicule.

## Private Desire

**Pack status**: firstness, fairness, not being the baby, being seen in “real” work.

## Fear / Wound

Exclusion; ridicule; being **underfoot**; being measured only by older kids’ bodies.

## Form Of Power

Pack rhythm; rumor; imitation; temporary alliances; **cousin economics** of shade and path.

## Relationship To Matriarchal Line

Audience-in-training for women’s governance; corrected by **any** adult woman with a free hand.

## Relationship To Binding

Binding year as **rumor, pride, wrong summaries** circulating among children—never accurate, always emotional.

## Relationship To Red River Trade World

Sugar gossip, cloth glimpses, “visitor weather”—**child scale**. No French POV.

## Relationship To Great Raft / River Landscape

Shared rules about **bank play**, slick mud, where splashing is allowed; river as **competition surface** (skipping stones) not commerce metaphor.
