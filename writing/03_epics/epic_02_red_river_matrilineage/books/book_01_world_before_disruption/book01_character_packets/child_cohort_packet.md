# Character Packet — Child cohort (collective)

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Identity

Children and young adolescents who share paths, chores, insults, and imitation — not a single “symbol child.”

## Book Function

Makes the village **lived-in**; prevents granddaughter POV from becoming solitary genius loneliness.

## Public Role

Runs messages, squabbles over sunny patches, repeats adult phrases wrong, tests edges of women’s quiet.

## Private Desire

Varies by child — default: **status within the pack** (fairness, firstness, shame).

## Fear / Wound

Ridicule; exclusion from “real” work; being treated as baby.

## Form Of Power

Pack rhythm; rumor; imitation as learning.

## Relationship To Matriarchal Line

They are the **audience-in-training** for women’s governance.

## Relationship To Binding

Binding year as rumor, pride, and half-true explanations circulating among children.

## Relationship To Red River Trade World

Trade arrives as gossip, sugar, strange cloth — child-scale.
