# Character Packet — Granddaughter / young heroine (name OPEN)

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Identity

Pre-raid child in the matriarchal household. **Name: OPEN.** POV-capable: **close child lens** preferred—body, mistake, appetite, overheard half-truths. She is **not** “the future” as abstraction; she is a **full child**: proud, observant, **sometimes wrong**, attached to **child-world** (pack, sunny patches, pebbles, shells), measured by women, tied to **binding future** through prep she half understands.

## Book Function

Reader must **love her as a person** before rupture—pride, wrongness, dignity, tenderness—so loss has weight. She carries **misread** that is still **story-useful** (binding year as height / “real job”) without wedding fantasy. She must **not** read as wise beyond age; **one** reflective beat per scene max; prefer **action + sensation**.

## Public Role

Learns knots; runs messages; competes with cousins; tests boundaries around women’s quiet work; performs **obedience with teeth** (grin wiped too late—Sample 002). She is the **visible third** of the matriarchal line in motion.

## Private Desire

**Place**: to be measured against something that matters—not only longer legs. To have **hands that matter** in the rooms where women go quiet. To **step into** grandmother’s open tilt without being underfoot.

## Fear / Wound

Laughed at; **unimportant** in women’s quiet; ordinary shame of failed loops; cousin jealousy; fear that **fast** is the only coin adults respect.

## Form Of Power

Observation; imitation; **small disobediences** that return to obedience; pocket-objects and tactile memory; **bad loops on purpose** (child tactic—Sample 002) as limited power.

## Relationship To Matriarchal Line

Watched and weighed “like a pot before heat”; taught by **hands** not speeches; grandmother’s pause/tap/two-finger correction as **love grammar**.

## Relationship To Binding

Half-understands binding year as **height / real job / prize-language corrected**—must stay **child-true**. Cord on peg out of reach = **desire geometry**. Mother's coil marker = **double meaning** she feels but does not over-explain.

## Relationship To Red River Trade World

Visitors mean sugar twists, trade cloth, jokes not funny yet; she reads eagerness as **weather**—not as ethnography. **No** French POV; no named colonial agents.

## Relationship To Great Raft / River Landscape

Bank mud, shells, river sound as **play and threat edge**; cousin’s pebble ripples; **path where visitors widen the day**. She learns **where not to go** on the water as **rule-of-thumb**, not engineering lesson. **No Shreve / Shreveport / port future** in her thoughts—only **size, cold, speed, mud**.
