# Book 01 character map overview

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Book 01 must make the reader deeply invested in **Native people**, **households**, **children**, **women’s authority**, **family futures**, **binding**, and **village life** before rupture.

The reader should **love the people before the raid**.

## Book 01 character rule

Every major character must serve at least one of:

- matriarchal power
- reader attachment
- binding future
- child-world
- village life
- kinship tension
- Red River landscape
- survival knowledge
- hidden pressure
- emotional rupture

## Native-only rule

Book 01 has no developed non-Native characters.
