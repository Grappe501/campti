# Family Line Historical Threading

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

The family line should carry the reader through major issues of each era in a realistic way.

## Rule

The family should not magically appear at every historical event.

Instead, major historical issues should affect the family through:

- land
- law
- marriage
- trade
- war
- records
- race
- language
- religion
- migration
- children
- household survival
- public office
- economic change
