# Draft Quality Target

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Target

Rough drafts should aim for approximately 75% production quality.

## Meaning

A draft at this level should have:

- clear scene purpose
- strong sensory world
- historically grounded material detail
- visible character desire
- relationship movement
- matriarchal power movement
- emotional turn
- continuity with the book spine
- no obvious canon contradiction
- no generic placeholder prose

## Not Required Yet

- final line polish
- perfect historical certainty
- final chapter order
- final dialogue refinement
- final cultural terminology
- final pacing

## Requirement

Any uncertain historical or cultural claim must remain flagged for review.
