# Destined Union Engine

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Every major union should feel destined through time, but realistic.

Destiny should not mean coincidence or romance fantasy.

Destiny should emerge from:

- geography
- trade routes
- kinship obligations
- political need
- family strategy
- survival pressure
- grief
- widowhood
- alliance
- children
- land
- law
- historical timing
- women’s long planning
- men’s public roles colliding with women’s continuity work

## Union Design Questions

For every major union, answer:

| Union | Woman’s need / power | Man’s role / system | Why they converge | What history pressures | What the union creates | What it costs |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
