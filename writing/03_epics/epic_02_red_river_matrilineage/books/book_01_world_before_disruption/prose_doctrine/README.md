# Prose and narrative doctrine — Book 01 (series-facing)

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Craft rules for **story and prose** passes: immersive scale, nature-rich setting, historical density through lived experience, character depth, matriarch power narratives, destined-union logic, parallel convergence, pacing, scene texture, draft quality. **Not** manuscript drafting in this folder. **Not** `ch_###` folders. **Not** `writing/01_canon/`.

## Human direction (summary)

The novel should feel **huge, detailed, beautiful, character-driven**: dense material and historical detail, vast nature, deep setting and characters, interwoven family history, major historical issues through the line, women-centered matriarchal narratives, unions that feel **inevitable** through time, pressure, geography, kinship, politics, survival.

High-level craft traits (not imitation of any author or show): technical/logistical specificity, lyrical landscape presence, generational scope, deep backstory, realistic historical threading, parallel storylines converging through family, power, and place.
