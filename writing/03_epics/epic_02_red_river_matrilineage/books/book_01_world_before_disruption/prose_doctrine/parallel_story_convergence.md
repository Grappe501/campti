# Parallel Story Convergence

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

The story should weave matriarchal narratives in parallel with the Grappe men and other male historical interfaces until the lines collide.

## Structure

The women’s stories should not begin only when men arrive.

Women should have momentum before union.

Men should enter as historical force, pressure, opportunity, danger, interface, or collision.

## Convergence Pattern

1. Woman has her own power path.
2. Man moves through a historical system.
3. Place / trade / law / war / kinship pushes them toward the same point.
4. Their union changes the family line.
5. The woman’s power adapts through the union rather than disappearing into it.

## Rule

The union should never erase the woman’s story.
