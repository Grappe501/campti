# Prose Pacing Rules

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Big Prose Rule

The prose can be huge, but every scene must move story, relationship, power, or world.

## Slow Is Allowed When

- the reader is falling in love
- a place is becoming real
- a relationship is deepening
- a matriarch’s power is revealed
- a historical system is entering through lived detail
- the binding future is becoming emotionally believable

## Slow Is Not Allowed When

- the description repeats information
- the scene has no transfer
- the detail is decorative only
- the character does not change
- the power relationship does not move
- the reader does not gain attachment or dread

## Chapter-Level Test

At the end of every chapter, identify:

- what changed
- who gained or lost power
- what the reader loves more
- what future feels more real
- what hidden pressure gained retroactive meaning
