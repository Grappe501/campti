# Matriarch Narrative Doctrine

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Governing Principle

Every major matriarch should have her own narrative of power.

The story should show how she enters power, learns power, uses power, protects power, transfers power, and adapts power.

## For Each Matriarch, Track

- first image
- first act of authority
- relationship to land
- relationship to children
- relationship to men
- relationship to trade
- relationship to marriage/binding
- relationship to memory
- what she protects
- what she sacrifices
- what she misunderstands
- how she changes the line
- what survives through her

## Core Rule

Women are not side characters to male history.

Men may be the recorded names, but women are the continuity engine.
