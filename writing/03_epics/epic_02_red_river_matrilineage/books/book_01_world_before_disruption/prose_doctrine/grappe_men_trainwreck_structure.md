# Grappe Men Trainwreck Structure

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Track how the Grappe men and other male historical figures collide with the women’s existing power lines.

“Trainwreck” means convergence under historical force: not random romance, but lives driven together by systems, place, crisis, ambition, survival, law, land, and family need.

## Male Historical Interface Pattern

| Male Figure | Historical System He Carries | Woman / Matriarchal Line He Collides With | Why Collision Happens | What Union Creates | What Woman Preserves / Gains / Risks |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Rule

The man may be the public-facing historical marker.

The woman must remain the power-continuity center.
