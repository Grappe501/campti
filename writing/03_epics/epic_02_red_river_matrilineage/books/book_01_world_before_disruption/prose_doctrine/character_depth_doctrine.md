# Character Depth Doctrine

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Every major character should feel like they could carry their own novel.

## Character Development Standard

Major characters need:

- public role
- private wound
- desire
- fear
- contradiction
- humor
- skill
- relationship network
- family role
- power role
- memory
- secret or withheld truth
- future they imagine
- future history denies or transforms

## Backstory Rule

Backstory should be revealed through pressure, not biography dumps.

## Ensemble Rule

The reader should love many people before the raid.

The village must not exist only to be destroyed.
