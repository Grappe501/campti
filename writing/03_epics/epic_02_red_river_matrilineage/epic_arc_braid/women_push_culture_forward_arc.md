# Women Push Culture Forward Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Track how women in the epic are pushed, challenged, or forced into positions ahead of their time.

## Governing idea

The women are not passive carriers of lineage.

They are cultural accelerants.

They push households, law, public life, politics, food, survival, and identity forward.

Sometimes men resist them.

Sometimes men push them forward.

Sometimes both happen in the same marriage.
