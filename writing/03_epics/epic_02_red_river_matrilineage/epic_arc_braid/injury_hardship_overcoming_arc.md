# Injury / Hardship / Overcoming Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

Across the epic, Grappe men should show physical injury, emotional trauma, illness, hardship, and genetic or inherited difficulty that they fight to overcome.

The point is not suffering for spectacle.

The point is endurance, leadership, and the cost of becoming useful to others.

## Rules

- Do not glamorize injury.
- Do not make men invincible.
- Show pain, limitation, pride, anger, humor, and adaptation.
- Injury should expose character.
- The women should not merely nurse the men; they challenge, confront, and redirect them.
