# Marriage Volatility / Partnership Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

Do not make marriages butterflies and flowers.

The major unions should be volatile, hard-nosed, full of fights, respect, pressure, and purpose.

These people are barely hanging on at times, but they recognize they are meant for something larger than personal gain.

## Rules

- Conflict must be earned.
- Respect must survive the fight.
- Women must challenge men directly.
- Men must not patronize women or let them stay small.
- Men push wives toward front-line cultural positions.
- These couples should feel ahead of their time.
