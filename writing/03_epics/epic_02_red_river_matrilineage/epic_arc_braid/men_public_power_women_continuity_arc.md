# Men’s Public Power / Women’s Continuity Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Track the **split** between **public-facing male roles** (law, trade interface, marshal myth, campaign) and **continuity engines** often carried through **women’s labor, memory, and refusal** — without reducing either side to stereotype.

## Pattern (PROPOSED)

| Era | Public male surface | Continuity female / household engine | Collision type |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | traders / outsiders visible | village women’s system | outside pressure vs internal law |
| 02–04 | colonial legal economy | household adaptation | dependence vs agency |
| 08–09 | badge / office / camera | kitchen + partnership volatility | myth vs truth |

## Rules

- Men can be wrong, afraid, or taught.
- Women can be wrong, afraid, or taught.
- **Continuity is not “virtue”;** it is **work**.
