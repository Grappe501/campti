# Family Against History Arc

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Track how **households absorb** wars, laws, migrations, booms, busts — and how **history misnames** what women and children already survived.

## Core moves

- **History arrives as a letter, a law, a price, a rumor** — rarely as a speech.
- **Family decisions** are economic before they are ideological.
- **Children** carry costs adults cannot fully translate.
