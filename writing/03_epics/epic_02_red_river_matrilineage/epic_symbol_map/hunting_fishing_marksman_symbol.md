# Hunting / fishing / marksman symbol

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

Hunting and fishing should show Grappe men providing food, reading land and water, and becoming expert marksmen and great landsmen across generations.

## Symbolic functions

- provision
- land knowledge
- discipline
- danger
- masculinity under pressure
- family survival
- food before money
- injury risk
- moral relationship to land

## Rule

Do not make hunting only sport.

It is survival, skill, family duty, and identity.
