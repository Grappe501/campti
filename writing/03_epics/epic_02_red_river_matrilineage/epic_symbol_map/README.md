Canon status: PROPOSED

## Purpose

Track **symbols, motifs, metaphors, colors, silences, nature cues, pace, mood, injuries, marriage pressure, objects, and food memory** across **Books 01–09** so choices stay **intentional**, not decorative.

**Human rule:** the reader earns depth. These files are **operator maps**, not prose explanations. Do not paste thesis paragraphs into scenes.
