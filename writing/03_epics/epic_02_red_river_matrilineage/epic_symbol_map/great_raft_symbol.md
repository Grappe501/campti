# Great Raft symbol

Canon status: PROPOSED pending research

## Symbolic meaning

The Great Raft symbolizes:

- obstruction
- protection
- delayed movement
- old systems holding water back
- landscape as destiny
- natural power before engineered power
- commerce waiting
- local knowledge versus outsider “improvement”
- Campti promise
- Shreveport shift
- family investment broken by altered geography

## Book 01 meaning

Ancient water-world **reality**.

The raft is **not a symbol yet** to the characters. It is simply how the world works.

## Later meaning

The same obstruction that shaped old life becomes part of the commercial dream and later family disappointment.

## Rule

Do not explain the symbol early.

Let the reader learn the **landscape** first.
