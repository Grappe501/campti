# Symbol Depth Rules

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Governing rules

1. **Earn depth:** one vivid, embodied beat beats three explanatory sentences.
2. **No decoder ring:** if a schoolteacher could lecture the symbol, cut the lecture; keep the object.
3. **Recurrence changes meaning:** same image + same meaning = mistake.
4. **Power first:** symbols ride **choice and cost**, not wallpaper.
5. **Research leash:** community-specific claims route through research logs, not vibes.
6. **Child POV:** symbols must remain **child-logical** when carried by children.

## Fail modes

- Thesis paragraphs disguised as description.
- Repeated catchphrases as “theme reminders.”
- Trauma spectacle.
- Food magic without labor.
