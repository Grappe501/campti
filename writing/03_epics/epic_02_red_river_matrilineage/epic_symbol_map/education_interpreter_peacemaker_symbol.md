# Education / interpreter / peacemaker symbol

Canon status: PROPOSED

## Human direction

All Grappes are educated in whatever ways are available in their time.

They excel as interpreters, liaisons, peacemakers, and bridges between people.

## Education means

Not only school.

Education may include:

- language
- land knowledge
- trade knowledge
- law
- church records
- military knowledge
- hunting/farming skill
- political literacy
- public service
- reading people
- knowing when not to speak

## Symbolic function

The family survives because it learns systems faster than systems can erase it.
