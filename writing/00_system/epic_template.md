# Epic template (reference)

## Epic folder layout

Under `03_epics/epic_##_<slug>/`:

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `epic_overview.md` | Why this epic exists; thematic engine; scope across books. |
| `epic_timeline.md` | Cross-book chronology; major eras or phases. |
| `epic_arc.md` | Large-scale character and plot movements; long mysteries. |
| `epic_questions.md` | Open questions spanning multiple books; promotion rules to canon. |

## Subfolder

- `books/` — `book_##_<slug>/` per volume in sequence.

## Cross-cutting concerns

- Anything that affects **more than one book** belongs at epic level first, then distill into per-book files.
- **PROPOSED:** epic-level beats must not be treated as canon until reflected in `01_canon/` or explicitly confirmed in team notes.
