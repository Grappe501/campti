Coincoin’s descendants were central to the Cane River Creole community. Her son Augustin Metoyer donated land for St. Augustine, and her family became tied to the enduring Isle Brevelle community. Modern summaries often blur details or over-credit her for places her sons later developed, but strong historical treatments make the distinction clear: Coincoin laid the foundation, and her children and descendants expanded it.
