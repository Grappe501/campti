# CHAPTER 1 ENGINE (SCENE-LEVEL EXECUTION)

## Opening Architecture

- time window: pre-dawn through first-light
- opening action: work already underway
- opening tone: competent order with subtle imbalance
- no setup lecture before embodied action

## Chapter Beat Spine (10 Beats)

1. pre-dawn recognition (elder notices first anomaly)
2. labor allocation shift (daughter adjusts routine)
3. child witness cue (silence or withheld answer)
4. practical task under pressure (younger near-misstep)
5. kin exchange with strain marker
6. environmental confirmation signal
7. traveler/intelligence entry with omission
8. continuity-first household decision
9. evening compression and unresolved unease
10. closing hook: pressure increases, order still standing

## Required Beat Payload

Each beat must include:

1. one physical action
2. one environmental signal
3. one social read
4. one hidden cognition cue
5. one pressure carry into the next beat

## Tension Method

Layer in this order:

1. small wrongness
2. recognition
3. interpretive mismatch
4. social consequence
5. unresolved unease

Do not jump directly to spectacle.

## Forbidden Constructions

- exposition block at start
- modern emotional-analysis narration
- sudden catastrophic event without prior signals
- decorative sensory writing detached from consequence

## Minimum Scene Validation

Before accepting a scene, verify:

- does this show a functioning society?
- does the matrilineal line act (not just appear)?
- does environment force at least one decision?
- does the scene end with continuity still possible but more costly?

## Chapter Exit Condition

End Chapter 1 with:

- continuity still intact
- pressure clearly rising
- no full release of core threat
- forward pull into Chapter 2 movement
